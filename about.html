About

By Trials Riders, For Trials Riders

Who We Are

Shin Dig Media is a bike trials media brand built by riders, for riders — covering the sport across YouTube, podcast, live events, and beyond.

Founded in 2020 by Matt Pengelly, the organisation grew from a lockdown connection initiative into a recognised trials community entity. Our mission is to elevate the sport, expand its audience reach, and ensure top-tier riders receive the recognition they deserve.

The Name

Every cyclist knows it — that moment when the pedal strikes your shin. It's universal, it's painful, and it connects every rider from beginner to pro. That spirited, shared experience is the foundation of everything we do.

Our Philosophy

We showcase riding across all competitive levels and street sessions because bike trials isn't just about the top riders — it's about an entire community of people pushing their own limits.

All merchandise profits reinvest directly into content production and community growth.
