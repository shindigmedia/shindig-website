FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is The Shin Dig?

The Shin Dig is a brand dedicated to the sport of bicycle trials — covering the sport across YouTube, podcast, live events, merchandise, and beyond.

How long does it take to get my stuff?

All of our items are made to order. EU stock takes up to 7 working days and US stock takes up to 14 working days.

Why don't you just stock the items yourselves?

Matt and Tom have full-time jobs outside the business. We produce podcasts and YouTube content, so stocking inventory ourselves would spread us too thin and harm the quality of the media we put out.

Why does shipping vary so much?

We work with multiple suppliers worldwide. Each supplier has minimum shipping costs depending on which products are ordered.

Can I submit my design?

Yes! We welcome new concepts and ideas representing the trials community and would love to promote community-created content. Get in touch via our socials.

I would like a product you don't have — can you make it?

We can source various products. If something's unavailable, email us — chances are someone else is probably thinking the same thing.

What is the future for The Shin Dig?

We aim to continually invest in and promote the sport because it's a unique discipline deserving global exposure. We're always working on new content, events, and ways to grow the community.
